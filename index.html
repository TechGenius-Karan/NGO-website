About us


Welcome to WellnessHarbour Foundation, where compassion meets action in the realm of healthcare. We're on a mission to bring quality healthcare within reach of every individual, regardless of their circumstances. Our journey is fueled by a deep commitment to collaboration, innovation, and unwavering dedication. At Wellness Harbour, we believe in the power of community and the transformative impact of accessible healthcare. Join us as we navigate the seas of healthcare equity, guided by the beacon of hope and healing.

Our Mission


Our mission at WellnessHarbour Foundation is driven by a commitment to advocating for healthcare equity, empowering communities, and fostering innovation. Through collaborative efforts and strategic initiatives, we aim to address the systemic barriers to healthcare access, promote health literacy, and empower individuals to become active agents in their own health journey. Our vision is grounded in the belief that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, regardless of their background or circumstances.


The WellnessHarbour Foundation focuses on:

  • Advocating for healthcare equity and access
  • Empowering communities through education and resources
  • Driving innovation in healthcare delivery
  • Promoting preventive care and health awareness
  • Collaborating with stakeholders to address systemic healthcare disparities
