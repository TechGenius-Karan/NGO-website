Our Initiatives


At WellnessHarbour Foundation, our initiatives are designed to meet the healthcare needs of our communities. We establish community health clinics, run educational programs, and host medical camps to improve health and well-being of individuals, families, and communities. Our goal is to provide quality healthcare, raise awareness, and collaborate with partners for sustainable solutions.


Initiative Description Status
Community Health Clinics Establishing clinics in underserved areas to provide primary healthcare services. Ongoing
Health Education Programs Conducting workshops and seminars to promote health literacy and awareness. Planned
Medical Outreach Camps Organizing medical camps to provide free healthcare services to remote communities. Upcoming
Telemedicine Services Introducing telemedicine platforms to facilitate remote consultations and medical advice. Under Development
Mental Health Awareness Campaigns Launching campaigns to reduce stigma and increase awareness about mental health issues. Planned
